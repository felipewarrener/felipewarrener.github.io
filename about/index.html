About Me

I am a 19 year-old security researcher and information security enthusiast from the United Kingdom, I am interested in all areas of hacking but some of my favourites are:

  • Reverse-engineering
  • Binary exploitation
  • Web/Infrastructure penetration-testing

I started looking into computer security after being fascinated by cheaters I encountered in online games, and I am driven by the challenge of making technology work in ways not originally intended.

I am always striving to learn more develop my skills, I occasionally play in CTF tournaments and WarGames as I like a challenge.

I like reading about all things computers, I’m fascinated by Operating Systems, Networks, Software Architecture and Computer Science and work full-time as a software developer.
